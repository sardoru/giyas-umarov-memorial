Complete Publications & Bibliography

Monographs, research papers, and third-party references documenting a lifetime of scientific contribution

Monographs & Books

  • 1970

    Beta-Spectrographs with Permanent Magnets

    A.A. Abdurazzakov, G.M. Gromov, G.Ya. Umarov — Monograph on beta-spectroscopy instrumentation developed at JINR Dubna

  • 1973

    Biruni, Copernicus, and Modern Science

    G.Ya. Umarov — Drawing intellectual lineage from al-Biruni to Copernicus to modern physics. Later translated to English as "At the Crossroads of the Millennium" (2001)

  • 1974

    Solar Energy

    G.Ya. Umarov, A.G. Ershov — Published in Moscow. Comprehensive overview of solar energy science and technology

  • 1976

    Use of Low-Potential Solar Installations

    G.Ya. Umarov et al. — Practical engineering guide for low-temperature solar thermal systems

  • 1977

    Theory and Calculation of Heliotechnical Concentrating Systems

    G.Ya. Umarov, R.A. Zakhidov, A.A. Vainer — Definitive theoretical treatment of solar concentrator design and analysis
